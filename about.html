Miguel Tomás Gomes

UX Writer & Content Designer

Based in Lisbon, Portugal

I've always been captivated by how words can reframe how we see the world. This fascination naturally drew me to Linguistics, where I dove deep into the mechanics of language.

When I transitioned into tech companies, I realized I was sitting at the intersection of two worlds I loved: the precision of language and the complexity of technology.

What truly transformed my approach was discovering the power of data-driven decisions. In a field where everyone has opinions about what "sounds better," data became my compass.

Outside of work, you'll find me wandering through Lisbon's neighborhoods or shooting analog photography.
